Delivery Policies

General Information: All customer orders re subject to pet availlability. In case an ordered pet is not availlable at the time of order, we will notify you and refund you the total amount of your order. We will use your original method of payment to refund.

Delivery Location: Pets offered on our website are only available in counties where our stores are located. Therefore, you can inquire from your nearest store to see wheher your favorite pet is availlable.

Delivery Time: We we always provide an estimated delivery time on every order made. This time commences from the date of shipping and not the date of order. Deliver times are used for guidance only an they are subject to your acceptance and approval of your order.

Shipping Costs: Shipping Costs are based on the number and type of pet ordered. This is in addition to your selected method of delivery.

Return Policies

Refunds Permitted: Return will only be permitted if you received a pet in bad condition e.g. sick or dead.

Refunds Period: We only give a maximum of 3 days for any pet.
